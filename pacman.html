Pacman Team Deathmatch

My first year semester 1 project.
Made in S2D.

Objectives

As a team of two, eat more munchies than the other team within a time limit. Team up to kill an opponents pacman to send them out of the match temporarily. Collect power-ups to give your team an advantage.

Pacman Combat

Attack an opponents pacman from the sides or back to temporarily kill them. After a while, they will respawn at their side of the map.

Power-ups

Occationally, power-ups will appear on the mpa for anyone to collect. For example, the spaceship makes your pacman move faster, and the key makes munchies regenerate faster.

Development

Made using Stafforshire University's OpenGL-based S2D framework. Code written using Visual Studio 2019.
