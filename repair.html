Survive & Repair

My team's project for Global Game Jam 2020.
While we didn't have the chance to finish it, we did create the basics of gameplay.
The Jam was themed around "repair", so the idea was that the player would attack by building and repairing defences.
Made in Unity, in 48 hours.

The House

The house (designed by teammate Max and built by me) is the main space in which the game takes place. With four rooms, there are plenty of places to retreat to.

Repair Gun

Instead of shooting enemies, you shoot a healing laser (made by teammate Jon, and me), which keeps your defences running (only turrets could be implemented in time). It uses scrap/money (by me) collected from enemies when they are killed.

Enemies

In the form of zombies. They use a navmesh pathfinding system downloaded from the Unity store (implemented by Jon) to traverse the house in order to attack you. If there are defences set up, they will target those instead.

Showcase

A gameplay demo recorded by one of my teammates Jon can be watched here.

Summary

I would've liked to have worked for longer on it, to at least get the wave system working. Mainly, I regret not doing enough work on the game, but due to my lack of experience with Unity I likely could not have contributed much more than I did. Overall, however, it was a good learning experience.

VS2019, Unity, C#.
